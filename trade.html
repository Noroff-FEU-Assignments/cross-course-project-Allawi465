How trades work

1

SELECT YOUR GAME

Fill out our trading form below and choose what game you want to trade.

The selected game has to be in stock on our website.

You can also choose to recieve store credit on your trade(s).

2

Send the game to us

Pack your game in an sealed envelope or package and send it to our address.

Remember to write your name on the package as well

3

RECEIVE YOUR GAME

In 3 - 5 business days you will receive your traded game or the earned store credit.
